Our System

The KUKA IIWA 14 is integrated with a Motive Optitrack system for 3D muscle image scanning. Our setup assists in probing, motion tracking, and real-time monitoring of human muscle activity, providing accurate biomechanical insights.

Robotic Assistance

The lightweight and sensitive KUKA IIWA 14 assists with safe probing and guided positioning for scanning.

3D Tracking

Motive Optitrack ensures real-time 3D tracking of muscle motion and posture for precision alignment.

Data Capture

Seamless integration captures movement and muscle imaging, creating accurate datasets for analysis.

AI Insights

Machine learning algorithms provide predictive modeling and visualization of muscle performance.

About Our Work

Robotics + Human Physiology

Our research integrates robotic precision with advanced 3D tracking systems to study human muscle dynamics. With the KUKA IIWA 14's sensitivity and the Optitrack's accuracy, we create a safe and effective platform for probing, imaging, and analyzing muscular performance.
